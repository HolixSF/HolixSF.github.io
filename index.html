About Me

My name is Luis De Castro. I'm a software developer looking for new opportunities and learning the newest technologies in this ever changing industry.

Ever since I was a kid I loved to create and build things. I played with Legos a lot and I believe thats where my passion and creativity came from. I then moved onto building and modifying cars during my teens. Taking something so ordinary and transforming it with your own style into something cool is the best best feeling in the world. From classic imports all the way to modern racecars, I love them all.

Transitioning into software development means I can take that same passion and creativity and create something meaningful. I want to take a simple idea and transform it into something awesome.

I'm happy to work on the next big thing with you so don't hesitate to get in contact with me. You can usually find me either at some coffee shop, drinking coffee and coding away at the next project or cruising in my 1974 BMW 2002.
